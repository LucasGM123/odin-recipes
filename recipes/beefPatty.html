Odin's Beef Patty

Description

This fresh beef patty dish was created by the God of Spices and it is said that only the chosen few will be able to finish a serving of this humongous dish. Follow the instructions below to have some of the best and authentic Jamaican beef patties you'll ever taste.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Jamaican jerk Seasoning
  • Salt And Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)

  2. Prepare pastry: Combine flour, curry powder, and salt in a bowl. Cut in margarine and shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in water until mixture forms a ball. Shape dough into a log and cut it into 10 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a 6-inch circle (approximately 1/8-inch thick) on a lightly floured surface; set aside.

  3. Spoon equal amounts of filling onto each pastry circle. Fold over and press edges together, making a half-circle. Use the back of a fork to seal edges, then brush tops with beaten egg. Place pastries on a baking sheet.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 30 minutes.
