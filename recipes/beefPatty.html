Odin's Beef Patty

Description

This fresh lasagna dish was created by the God of fire and it is said that only the chosen few will be able to finish a serving of this humongous dish. Follow the instructions below to have some of the best lasagna you'll ever taste.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Lasagna Pasta Noodles
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Crushed Tomatoes

Directions

  1. Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.

  2. boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready. We're using ricotta, shredded mozzarella, and parmesan -- like the mix of tomatoes, this 3-cheese blend gives the lasagna great flavor!

  3. A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until you have three layers and have used up all the ingredients.

  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
